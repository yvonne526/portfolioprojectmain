PROJECT 2

MASTER GAMER

Purpose of this project

  • It acts as a bridge between game developers and testers by addressing challenges in a streamlined way.
  • It helps game developers get geniune feedbacks from testers who test their games on the same paltform.

PROCESS

Step 1

  1. Do research on the problems that most game developers face, e.g., game developers facing challenge in finding reliable testers who give valuable feedback to improve on their game before release.

Step 2

  1. Came up with an idea of designing a collaborative website that brings game developers and testers in one place for easy way of addresssing challenges in a streamlined way.

Step 3

  1. Deploy: Users to interact with the website seamlessly.
